Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Wound Care AI ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the application.

Information We Collect

  • Name and account information.
  • Email address.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide AI-assisted wound assessment.
  • Display wound healing predictions.
  • Maintain patient history.

  • Improve application performance and security.

Use of Uploaded Images

Images uploaded by users may be securely retained and used to improve the accuracy, reliability, and future development of the artificial intelligence models. Whenever possible, data used for research or model improvement is processed in a manner that protects patient privacy and confidentiality.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

The application may use secure cloud infrastructure and authentication services necessary for operating the platform. These services process data only as required to provide the application's functionality.

Medical Disclaimer

Wound Care AI provides AI-assisted informational wound assessments only. The application does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Clinical decisions should always be made by qualified healthcare professionals.

Your Rights

Users may request correction or deletion of their personal information where applicable and subject to legal or regulatory requirements.
